# Weird Skience

A site by the [AVC CS Club](/cs-club/)

Come and explore the wonderful world of a STEM major at AVC!

While this is a site ran by the Computer Science Committee, all STEM students are welcome to contribute to this website. What you see here are the generous (and wacky) contributions made by said students.

Get to know us on a deeper and personal level. One minute someone could be talking about serious intellectual things that's happening in the world of science, and the next can talk about weird facts about the Owl; or something they're interested in at the moment. This is truly an outlet for self-expression, thoughts, interests, or just simple slice-of-life updates.

Well what are you waiting for? Get to clicking those links!
