# Getting back into Music Production by coding in Strudel
### by Maria Trezha Von Bargen

Personally and culturally music has been a significant part of my life. Singing and Karaoke, playing the violin and piano, or overhearing my uncle's obsession with european electronic/disco music late at night, and high school music production, for the most part it played a role in this current fascination I have with Strudel. A free, open-source, web-based live coding platform used to produce music in real time.

## Music and Me

I'm Filipino, enough said lol. 

In all seriousness, growing up in the Philippines includes hearing karaoke sessions wherever and whenever, and I am not an exception. I am fully into Karaoke and I will sing to my hearts content because a girl just wants to have fun. It is a little bit ironic since for years, one of my biggest insecurities was my voice. I already looked young physically, but hearing it vocally sent me over the edge sometimes and I would feel so embarrased and shy. Now that I have matured over the years, I feel more at peace about that insecurity, and have grown to like my voice thanks to Karaoke. Shout out to the portable mics they make for kids, I love it. Sincerly, a college student in their mid 20s.

I also have my background in performing music, with the violin and piano. I took violin lessons for 3 years and piano for 1.
